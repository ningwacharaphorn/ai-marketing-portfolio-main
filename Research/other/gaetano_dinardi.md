# Career Background: Gaetano DiNardi (Founder @ Marketing Advice)

Gaetano DiNardi is a B2B growth advisor and consultant who went from music producer and songwriter in New York City to one of the most respected SEO and demand generation minds in B2B SaaS.

- **Podcast:** [Future Search for SaaS: A New Era of Growth and Discovery](https://www.advancedwebranking.com/blog/saas-growth-discovery-in-ai-search-era) - How AI is changing where B2B SaaS buyers search and discover.
- **Official Site:** [marketingadvice.io](https://marketingadvice.io/) - Advisory and consulting practice for B2B SaaS growth.

### 🚀 Professional Timeline

#### 1. The Accidental Marketer (Early Career)
- **The Origin Story:** Gaetano began as a music producer and songwriter in New York City, working with major artists like Fat Joe and Shaggy. He stumbled into marketing through blogging about his music career and discovered his content was ranking in Google — entirely by accident.
- **The Pivot:** His SEO instincts caught the attention of iPullRank founder Mike King, who brought him into the agency world. He then moved into B2B SaaS at Pipedrive, where he built his foundational demand generation skills.

#### 2. The Operator Era — Nextiva & Sales Hacker (2015 – 2021)
- **The Growth Machine:** As Director of Demand Generation at Nextiva, Gaetano helped grow the company from ~$70M to $200M+ in revenue by building a powerful inbound engine driven by content, SEO, and YouTube — a channel he identified as underutilized in B2B.
- **The Community Builder:** As VP of Marketing at Sales Hacker, he grew the B2B sales community into a leading industry destination before its acquisition by Outreach.io, proving his ability to build audience-first, media-driven growth engines.

#### 3. The Advisory & Solo Era (2021 – Present)
- **The Practitioner-Consultant:** Gaetano founded Marketing Advice, an advisory practice helping 50+ B2B SaaS brands including Semrush, Gong, Demandbase, Aura, Cognism, and Teramind dominate competitive categories through AI-driven SEO, demand capture, and video-first organic strategy.
- **The AI-Era Pivot:** Named "AEO Voice to Watch in 2026" by Webflow, Gaetano has become one of the most vocal advocates for YouTube and video assets as core B2B visibility drivers in both traditional SERPs and AI-generated answers.

### 💡 Notable Background Insights
- **The Musician's Edge:** Gaetano credits his background in music production for his understanding of what makes content resonate. He approaches SEO and YouTube content the same way he approaches a record — with hooks, structure, and emotional clarity.
- **Strategic Insight for B2B SaaS:** Gaetano highlights that most B2B SaaS companies are dramatically underutilizing YouTube. His research shows that YouTube videos now surface alongside blog posts in Google SERPs and are increasingly cited in AI Overviews — meaning a single well-optimized video can generate compounding visibility across both human search and AI discovery simultaneously.
