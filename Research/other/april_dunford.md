### 🚀 Professional Timeline

#### 1. The Executive Leader (20+ Years)
- **The Practitioner:** April didn't start as a consultant; she spent decades as an executive at startups and giants like IBM, Siebel, and Janna Systems.
- **Acquisition Machine:** She has lead marketing and product teams through 6 acquisitions and dozens of product launches, giving her deep "battle-tested" experience.

#### 2. The Birth of "Obviously Awesome" (2019 – Present)
- **Framework Creator:** After seeing too many great products fail due to poor positioning, she developed her own 5-part framework to systematize how companies find their "Secret Sauce."
- **Global Consultant:** Founded Ambient Strategy to consult exclusively with B2B technology companies, becoming the go-to expert for VCs and CEOs worldwide.

#### 3. The Content & Strategy Era
- **Thought Leadership:** Beyond consulting, she has become a major voice in the B2B community through her keynote speeches and viral educational content.
- **SaaS Specialist:** Her work focuses on the unique challenges of tech, where the market moves fast and "buying context" is everything.

### 💡 Notable Background Insights
- **The "Context" Obsession:** April believes that humans are "meaning-making machines." If we don't tell them what category our product is in, they will guess—and they will usually guess wrong.
- **Strategic Insight for B2B SaaS:** For SaaS, April highlights that positioning is the "North Star" of content. If your video doesn't instantly communicate why you are the unique solution to a specific, high-value problem, you aren't just losing views—you're losing market share.
