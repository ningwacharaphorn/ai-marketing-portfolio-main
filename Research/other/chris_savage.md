# Career Background: Chris Savage (CEO & Co-founder @ Wistia)

Chris Savage is a visionary leader in the video marketing space, known for co-founding Wistia and championing a human-centric approach to B2B SaaS growth.

### 📚 Other Sources:
- **YouTube:** [Wistia](https://www.youtube.com/@wistia) - Lessons on video production, marketing strategy, and brand building.
- **Official Site:** [Wistia.com](https://wistia.com/) - Leading video marketing platform for business.

### 🚀 Professional Timeline

#### 1. The Creative Foundation (2006)
- **Founding Wistia:** Chris co-founded Wistia in his living room, bridging the gap between film production and technology to help businesses use video more effectively.
- **Early Vision:** Focused on providing deep analytics and a professional video player when most businesses were still figuring out how to host content online.

#### 2. The Debt-Free Pivot (2017 – 2020)
- **Sustainable Growth:** In a major strategic shift, Chris and his co-founder took on $17M in debt to buy out investors, choosing long-term sustainability over the "growth-at-all-costs" venture capital model.
- **Brand Affinity:** Pioneered the concept of "Brand Affinity," moving beyond simple awareness to focus on creating deep, emotional connections with audiences through high-quality episodic content.

#### 3. Human-Centric AI Strategy (2024 – Present)
- **Authenticity in the AI Era:** Chris advocates for using AI to handle routine tasks while doubling down on "Human-First" content. He believes human faces and personal trust are now high-value, scarce resources.
- **Lo-fi & High Trust:** Promotes the use of lo-fi, authentic video content for internal culture and external sales to build trust in an increasingly automated world.

---

### 💡 Notable Background Insights
- **Brand over Leads:** Chris argues that the future of B2B marketing is in long-form storytelling and creative content that builds a loyal community, rather than just chasing lead-gen forms.
- **Video as a Culture Tool:** He emphasizes that video should start internally. Using video for team updates and communication builds the "muscle" needed for successful external marketing.
- **Refined Taste is the Moat:** In a world flooded with AI-generated content, Chris believes that a brand's unique "taste" and creative judgment are its most defensible assets.
