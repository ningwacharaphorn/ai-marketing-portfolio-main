# Career Background: Eric Siu (Founder @ Single Grain & Leveling Up)

Eric Siu is a prominent marketing agency founder, podcaster, and investor, recognized for transforming a failing agency into a global growth marketing powerhouse.

### 📚 Other Sources:
- **Podcast:** [Marketing School Podcast](https://marketingschool.io/) - Daily marketing insights co-hosted with Neil Patel.
- **Podcast:** [Leveling Up with Eric Siu](https://www.levelingup.com/podcast/) - Interviews with world-class entrepreneurs and explorers of growth.
- **Agency:** [Single Grain](https://www.singlegrain.com/) - High-growth digital marketing agency for B2B & SaaS.

### 🚀 Professional Timeline

#### 1. The Bold Acquisition (2013)
- **The $2 Turnaround:** Eric acquired Single Grain, an SEO agency that was on the verge of bankruptcy, for only $2.
- **Resuscitation:** He successfully pivoted the agency from a struggling SEO shop into a full-service growth marketing agency serving top-tier clients like Uber and Salesforce.

#### 2. Building the Media Engine (2014 – 2020)
Eric focused on building "Content Sprouting" and community-based marketing:
- **Marketing School:** Partnered with Neil Patel to launch one of the most popular daily marketing podcasts in the world.
- **Leveling Up Brand:** Published the "Leveling Up" book, applying gaming mechanics (grinding, strategy, ranking up) to business and personal development.
- **Software Investments:** Invested in and scaled multiple SaaS products to enhance the marketing ecosystem.

#### 3. Strategic Growth & Masterminds (2020 – Present)
- **Content Sprouting Framework:** Mastered the art of repurposing one long-form video/podcast into hundreds of micro-assets for LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube.
- **Founders' Community:** Leads "Leveling Up Founders," an exclusive mastermind for high-net-worth entrepreneurs to share growth strategies.

### 💡 Notable Background Insights
- **Gaming Discipline:** Eric credits his success to his background as a hardcore gamer. He views business as the "ultimate game" where leveling up requires constant learning and strategic risk-taking.
- **The Practitioner's Edge:** Unlike typical theorists, Eric's advice is rooted in the daily operations of managing an agency that handles millions in ad spend and SEO for B2B giants.
