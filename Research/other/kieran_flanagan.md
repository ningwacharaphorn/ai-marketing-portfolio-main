# Career Background: Kieran Flanagan (SVP of Marketing @ HubSpot)

Kieran Flanagan is a prominent B2B marketing leader and growth strategist known for his "Media-First" and "Personality-Led" approach. He has been instrumental in scaling some of the world's most successful SaaS companies by moving beyond traditional playbooks toward creator-led growth.

### 🚀 Professional Timeline

#### 1. The Scaling Expert (Zapier Era)
- **The Growth Catalyst:** As the former CMO of Zapier, Kieran played a critical role in scaling the automation platform's marketing engine.
- **System Builder:** He focused on building efficient, high-growth systems that simplified complex technical products for a broad audience.

#### 2. The Global Leader (HubSpot Era — Present)
- **The Strategic Visionary:** Currently serving as SVP of Marketing at HubSpot, Kieran leads a massive global team of over 300 marketers.
- **The Media Architect:** He is the co-host of "Marketing Against the Grain," where he advocates for brands to act like media companies rather than just software vendors.
- **Strategic Direction:** He is responsible for the strategic direction of HubSpot’s growth, moving the brand from a traditional "Inbound" focus toward a modern "Media-First" ecosystem.

#### 3. The AI & Creator-Led Era (2024 – 2026)
- **Personality-Led Growth:** Kieran has become a vocal leader in the shift toward "Personality-Led Growth," arguing that in an AI-driven world, people trust individuals more than faceless corporate brands.
- **The AI Workflow Pivot:** In 2026, he has been at the forefront of integrating AI into marketing workflows, teaching teams how to use LLMs (like ChatGPT o3) to handle the manual "grind" of execution while doubling down on human authority and creativity.

### 💡 Notable Background Insights
- **Individual > Brand:** Kieran’s core philosophy is that modern algorithms on YouTube and social platforms favor individuals, not logos.
- **Media Moat:** He believes every B2B SaaS program should shift from generic blogging to creator-led programs to stay visible in AI-generated search results and build a "Media Moat" that AI cannot easily replicate.
- **Shift from Information to Authority:** As LLMs provide direct answers to informational queries, marketers must shift focus to creating unique awareness and authority that stands out.
- **AI as an Execution Assistant:** AI should be used to reduce administrative overhead, moving from "thinking" about tasks to actually "doing" them, such as drafting emails or creating presentation decks.
- **New Literacy (Prompting):** Treat prompting as a vital new skill; use advanced reasoning models for complex tasks and build custom GPTs to house proprietary frameworks.
