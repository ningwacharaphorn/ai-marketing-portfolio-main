### 💡 Strategic Takeaways from Alex Bass regarding Sam Oh:

Alex Bass highlights that Sam Oh’s recent strategies (via Ahrefs) are critical for anyone running a B2B SaaS marketing engine. The key insights are:

1. **YouTube as an "AI Trust Signal"**
   - YouTube is now one of the most referenced sources in **AI Overviews**. 
   - *Strategic Mandate:* If your SaaS brand isn't being mentioned or demonstrated by top creators on YouTube, you effectively "do not exist" in the eyes of AI search engines.

2. **Dominating Intent-Based Listicles**
   - Search queries are shifting toward comparison and discovery (e.g., "Best X for Y" or "X Alternatives").
   - To show up in AI-generated summaries, SaaS brands must be featured in these recommended listicles and high-authority sites.

3. **The "Affiliate-to-Visibility" Loop**
   - Affiliate programs are a powerful lever to ensure listicle sites cover your software. 
   - Brands shouldn't expect organic coverage without providing a clear incentive for reviewers and authority sites to feature them.

### 🎯 Summary of Sam Oh’s Approach (The Ahrefs Standard):

Based on the broader discussion in the SEO community:
- **Value Over Ranking:** Stop writing "fluff" just to rank. If the content doesn't provide genuine value or solve a specific user problem quickly, it fails.
- **Revenue-Driven SEO:** Focus resources on achievable SEO battles that directly propel company revenue, rather than chasing vanity metrics like backlinks alone.
