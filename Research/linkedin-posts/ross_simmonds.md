# LinkedIn Insights: Expert Analysis on Ross Simmonds’ Strategic Impact

**Topic:** “Memory” as the New Layer of Discovery (Beyond GEO vs SEO vs AEO)  
**Reference Source:** [SEO Week’s Post featuring Ross Simmonds](Https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ross-simmonds-kicked-off-day-3-the-ecosystem-share-7455256413846654976-bgOx)

### 💡 Strategic Takeaways from Ross Simmonds (via SEO Week):
Ross’ core insight is that the industry is over-rotating on naming debates (GEO vs. SEO vs. AEO) and missing the bigger shift: **AI systems are building memory over time**, and that memory will shape what gets discovered, recommended, and trusted.

1. **Stop debating labels—focus on what’s changing**
   - GEO/SEO/AEO discussions can distract from the real directional shift.
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Aim strategy at durable advantage, not terminology.

2. **“Memory” will shape discovery and decisions**
   - As AI tools learn user preferences and context, they’ll recommend based on accumulated history, not just a single query.
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Optimize for being remembered and repeatedly surfaced, not just “ranking.”

3. **Treat content like an investment, not output**
   - Content should compound over time and earn repeated distribution.
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Build assets that retain value (evergreen, reusable, insight-rich) instead of chasing volume.

4. **Measure what actually influences buyers**
   - The important question becomes: which content changes perception and drives decisions?
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Track downstream impact (pipeline influence, sales conversations, self-reported attribution) rather than vanity engagement alone.

5. **Thought leadership needs a quality reset**
   - “Too much of it is mediocre” → commodity takes won’t stick in a memory-driven world.
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Publish fewer, stronger points of view that are specific, evidence-backed, and repeatable.

6. **Don’t ignore new formats (e.g., YouTube Shorts)**
   - Short-form formats can shape awareness and recall at scale.
   - *Strategic Mandate:* Use format diversity to increase frequency of exposure and reinforce key ideas.

### 🎯 Summary of Ross Simmonds’ Approach (Memory-First Content Strategy):
Based on the post’s message:
- **Compounding content** wins over content volume.
- **Influence and recall** become the core success metrics.
- **Distinct points of view** matter more than generic “best practices.”
- **Multi-format distribution** (including short-form video) helps brands become familiar—and therefore remembered.
