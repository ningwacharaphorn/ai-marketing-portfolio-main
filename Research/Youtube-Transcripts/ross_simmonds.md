## Summary
This webinar outlines Ross Simmonds’ B2B content system for generating predictable leads by replacing the “create and pray” approach with a repeatable engine built on repurposing and distribution. The central idea is to produce high-quality “seed” content once, then systematically distribute and remix it across channels to earn attention repeatedly, compound reach, and nurture buyers throughout the funnel.

## Key points (the “predictable leads” workflow)
1. **Stop “create and pray”:** Consistent pipeline comes from a system—content that is intentionally distributed, not just published and hoped for.
2. **The Four E’s Framework (what makes content valuable):** Every asset should be at least one of:
   - **Educational**
   - **Engaging**
   - **Entertaining**
   - **Empowering**
3. **Core motto: “Create Once, Distribute Forever.”** Build a library by investing in strong pillar/seed content (webinars, podcasts, long-form videos), then repurpose it into many smaller assets for multiple platforms.
4. **The 4-step media engine model:** A repeatable content machine runs on:
   - **Research** (what the market actually cares about)
   - **Creation** (pillar content + supporting assets)
   - **Distribution** (systematic multi-channel publishing)
   - **Optimization** (improve based on performance + feedback)
5. **Reddit strategy for demand discovery + distribution:** Use search (including `site:reddit.com`) to find what resonates in target communities, then participate by adding value (not pitching) and sharing relevant resources.
6. **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO):** Optimize content to be favored by LLMs (ChatGPT/Claude) by using authoritative language, citing sources, and maintaining natural human flow so systems can confidently reference your material.
7. **Repurposing efficiency with AI tools:** Tools (e.g., Flowjin) can convert long-form content into clips, quotes, short posts, and blog drafts—accelerating output without restarting from scratch.
8. **Small-team playbook:** Start with one pillar content format (often a podcast or webinar series), interview experts to build goodwill and trust, and turn each conversation into a compounding library of micro-content that nurtures leads over time.

---
**Bottom Line:** Predictable B2B leads come from a distribution-first content system. Create high-quality seed content, repurpose it into dozens of assets, distribute intentionally across channels (including communities), and optimize—so your content compounds instead of expiring after one post.
